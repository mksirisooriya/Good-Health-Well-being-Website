Mental Health

Our emotional, psychological, and social well-being are all parts of our mental health. It influences our feelings, thoughts, and behaviors. It also influences how we respond to stress, interact with people, and make wise decisions.1. Every stage of life, from infancy and adolescence to maturity, is critical for mental health.

Why is mental well-being critical to general health? Both physical and mental well-being are crucial aspects of total health. Depression, for instance, raises the risk for a variety of physical health issues, especially chronic illnesses like diabetes, heart disease, and stroke. In a similar vein, having long-term medical issues might raise your chance of mental illness.2.
